Our Cabins

In addition to the main lodge building, Killarney Cabins has 2 private, self-contained housekeeping cabins with grand views facing the striking white quartzite mountains beyond.





Ginger's Cabin

Ginger's Cabin has 3 bedrooms, living room, kitchen, 3 piece washroom and a sunroom.

    Sleeping Arrangements

  • Bedroom 1: 1 Double Bed
  • Bedroom 2: 1 Double Bed
  • Bedroom 3: 1 Bunk Bed and 1 Cot

    Cabin Features

  • Fully Furnished
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Canoe, Adult life jackets and paddles
  • Sunroom (Ginger's Cabin ONLY)

    Amenities

  • Fully equipped kitchen with fridge, stove, coffee maker and toaster
  • Utensils and dishes
  • 3 piece washroom with shower
  • Furnishings, beds, pillows and light weight comforters
  • Stunning views of quartzite mountains
