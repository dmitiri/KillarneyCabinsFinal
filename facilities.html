Facilities

The lodge and cabins face Pine Island Channel of Georgian Bay and the Killarney Ridge of the South La Cloche Range. Small watercraft can navigate the bay, but care should be taken to avoid shoals due to the very low water level.

From the top of Killarney Ridge you can see the open waters of Georgian Bay, Manitoulin Island, and even the Bruce Peninsula.




The Killarney Provincial Park Office at George Lake is just 8 km east along Highway 637. For more information, visit the Ontario Parks Killarney page or the Friends of Killarney Park.

Outfitters in the area, such as Killarney Outfitters, provide canoe, kayak, and camping rentals, as well as outdoor gear.

The town of Killarney lies just 2 km west, with a general store, LCBO, restaurants, small shops, and a lively wharf where fishing boats bring in their catches.
